    Our Work Process

    • 01. Initiation
    • 02. Planning
    • 03. Execution
    • 04. Closure
    Identify a business need, problem, or opportunity and brainstorm ways that your team can meet this need, solve this problem, or seize this opportunity. Figure out an objective for your project, determine whether the project is feasible, and identify the major deliverables for the project.
    • Identifying deliverables
    • Identifying scope
    • Undertaking a feasibility study
    • Developing a business case
    Break down the larger project into smaller tasks, build your team, and prepare a schedule for the completion of assignments. Create smaller goals within the larger project, making sure each is achievable within the time frame. Smaller goals should have a high potential for success.
    • Creating a project plan
    • Gathering resources
    • Estimating budget and creating a financial plan
    • Creating workflow documents or process maps
    Now it’s time to get to work. The execution phase turns the plan into action. Keep work on track, organize team members, manage timelines, and make sure the work is done according to the original plan.
    • Creating tasks and organizing workflows
    • Briefing team members on tasks
    • Monitoring quality of work
    • Managing budget
    Provide final deliverables, release project resources, and determine the success of the project. Evaluate what did and did not work with the project.
    • Analyzing project performance
    • Analyzing team performance
    • Documenting project closure
    • Accounting for used and unused budget

    Some FAQ’s

    • What’s in the Fab Lab?
      Fab Lab contains various pieces of computer controlled fabrication equipment ranging from 3D printers, laser cutters and milling machines, e.t.c. not to mention a variety of electronic components and materials. Fab Lab also provides access to computers and easy-to-use design and manufacture software, capturing your ideas and turning them into reality at the press of a button. The Fab Lab is staffed by friendly highly-skilled technicians who can offer support and training in the use of the equipment. All we ask is that users share their ideas and knowledge and help fellow users to get the most out of the equipment and resources.
    • How do I get started?
      Whether you're a business, entrepreneur/inventor, school , part of a community group, or just a hobbyist, FabLab gives you the tools to bring your product ideas to life by educating users with the knowledge to develop new ideas, designs and products, allowing them to gain the technical skills to turn concepts into products. By bringing these elements together, FabLab is inspiring the next generation of entrepreneurs, engineers and manufacturing talent.
    • Who is Fab-Lab for?
      Fab-Lab enables anyone at any age, to experiment in turning ideas to tangible reality. Fab-Lab is looking for “makers” – for whom “do-it-yourself” is a motto; hobbyists who love robots, model airplanes, model building and more; professionals who love to do-it-themselves; artists; innovators in spirit and in occupation and any other individuals, young and old, who would like to “make” and would like to share from their experiences. Whether conceptual artists or “technology geeks”, university students, young or old, men or women – the Fab-Lab is available for anyone who has the passion to “make”.
    • How to become a member?
      If you are interested in becoming a member of FABlab and would like to get involved in all things fab! fill out the registration form (by clicking JOIN US above). It is completely free to register and this allows you to be kept upto date with all the courses and workshops that FABlab has to offer!
